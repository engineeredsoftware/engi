# Bitcode Protocol Thesis Notes

Status: non-canonical internal note.

## Thesis

Bitcode is the source-to-shares protocol for technical intelligence.

From source, Bitcode measures Reads, finds fit, synthesizes AssetPacks, records proof, and enables settlement through Exchange and Terminal surfaces.

## Product Commitments

- Source evidence must be bound before inference.
- Reads must be reviewable before fit search.
- Fit qualities must be explicit before settlement.
- AssetPacks are the stable output structure.
- Delivery mechanisms are wrappers over AssetPacks.
- Proof is a product requirement, not an afterthought.
- Terminal, Exchange, MCP, ChatGPT App, and connected interfaces are views/admissions over one protocol state.

## Engineering Commitments

- Prefer first-principles Bitcode names over compatibility names.
- Keep abstractions local, typed, and registry-backed.
- Remove non-Bitcode concepts unless precisely repurposed.
- Treat tests, generated proofs, and SPEC text as part of implementation.
