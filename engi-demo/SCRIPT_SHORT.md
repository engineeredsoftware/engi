# ENGI Demo Script — Short Version

Use this when you want the cleanest possible live explanation in ~60–90 seconds.

---

## Ultra-short thesis

ENGI turns engineering knowledge into something that is **openly writable**, **measurably readable**, **provable**, and **valuable**.

---

## 60–90 second script

> This demo shows the full ENGI loop end to end.
>
> First, the depot is **openly writable**: contributors can deposit engineering knowledge, and what becomes public is the commitment surface — hashes, metadata, measurement signals, and verification state.
>
> Second, it is **measurably readable**: a buyer facing a real production auth incident can issue a licensed query, and the system assembles a private, need-matched bundle instead of just dumping the whole corpus.
>
> Third, it is **provable**: the system emits public receipts, ranking explanations, conservation checks, policy binding, and provenance surfaces so the read is inspectable rather than magical.
>
> Finally, it is **valuable**: the demo ends on a benchmark showing that the licensed read improved the buyer's remediation system on a concrete production auth rollback task.
>
> So the claim is not just that knowledge was stored, and not just that it was retrieved — the claim is that the right engineering knowledge was licensed, proven, and measurably useful.
