Observed benchmark uplift

This section is intentionally separate from the core protocol flow above. It records an illustrative utility receipt showing buyer-system performance before vs after licensed bundle consumption. In the current prototype, these scores are operator-entered benchmark results in basis points, not an automatically computed benchmark harness, and they do not feed back into asset measurement.

Operator framing

Use this section only after the audience understands the core protocol behaviors. The claim here is buyer value: treatment minus baseline on a benchmark task after licensed consumption. The claim is not that ENGI currently auto-measures this lift or feeds it back into contributor scoring.
